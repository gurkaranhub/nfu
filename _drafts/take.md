*Description:*

Takes $$n$$ rows.

*Arguments:*

1 required: A number `n` specifying the number of lines to be taken. Write `n` to take the first `n`
and `+n` to take last `n`.

*Examples:*

```
nfu words -T 10
nfu words -T +10
```
